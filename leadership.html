Project Encephalon

I am the Founder and President of Project Encephalon , an international, trainee-led non-profit, organisation for neuroscience enthusiasts. I am also the Founder and Managing trustee of its parent organisation Project Encephalon Foundation.

Project Encephalon conducts various academic and non-academic neuroscience-related activities while accounting for the interdisciplinary needs of the field. All the activities conducted by Project Encephalon are always free to attend by anyone. Similarly, there is no membership fees to join Project Encephalon.

Project Encephalon was started as my personal goal on 24 June 2020, to learn and teach neuroscience to everyone who is interested in the field, regardless of their academic and socio-economic background.
